Description

This is my favorite reverse-sear steak recipe! Due to the nature of reverse-searing, this can be done with a frozen steak in a pinch. Just lower the temperature of the oven step and bake for longer!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cut of steak -- I like tri-tip
  • High smoke-point oil, like avocado or grapeseed
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Any other seasonings of your liking!

Steps

  1. Pat your steak dry with a paper towel
  2. Season the surface liberally with salt
  3. Bake in oven at 350F-ish for 12-ish minutes
  4. Heat up a cast-iron or other non-stick pan and add a tablespoon or two of oil
  5. Place your steak in the pan
  6. After steak is almost seared to your liking, add butter to pan along with any other seasonings if desired
  7. After steak is cooked to your liking, take out and let rest for 5 minutes
  8. Slice, plate, and enjoy!
